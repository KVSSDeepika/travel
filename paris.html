Paris, France

Nicknamed:
The City of Light

Culture:
Paris is known for its fashion, art, cafes, and history. French cuisine, the Louvre Museum, and iconic architecture like the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame shape its identity.

Top Attractions:
Eiffel Tower, Louvre, Champs-Élysées, Sacré-Cœur, Montmartre.

Best Time to Visit:
April to June or October to early November (pleasant weather and fewer crowds).
